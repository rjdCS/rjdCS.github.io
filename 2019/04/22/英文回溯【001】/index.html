英文回溯【001】
Tech investors include #MeToo clauses in start-up deals科技业投资者在与初创企业的交易中加入#MeToo条款

搭配查询网址：Linggle 10^12

个人学习笔记，如有侵权立即删除。

文章来源：FT中文网

[1]

Tech investors include #MeToo clauses in start-up deals

  • clause 条款
  • start-up 初创企业

英译中：
科技业投资者在与初创企业的交易中加入#MeToo条款。

仿写：
我将在与初创公司的后续合同中加入这些条款。
I will include this clauses in the subsequent contracts with start-ups.

[2]

(1) Tech investors are increasingly including ‘#MeToo’ clauses in deals with start-ups,(2) forcing entrepreneurs to disclose complaints about sexual harassment in the workplace, (3) as more women speak out about sexism in the industry.

(1)

  • increasingly 越来越多
  • in deals with (start-ups) 与初创公司达成的交易

(2)

  • V-ing 表伴随（同时）？动词ing的用法
  • forcing(force) (entrepreneurs) to (disclose complaints) 强迫(业家企)(to)(揭露投诉)
  • disclose 揭露
  • sexual harassment 性骚扰

(3)

  • as 表伴随？表原因？ as的用法
  • speak out (打破沉默地，公开地)说出
  • sexism 性别歧视

英译中：
(1)越来越多的科技行业投资者在于初创公司达成交易时要求增加“#MeToo”条款，(2)迫使创业者（企业家）揭露在工作场所中性骚扰的投诉，(3)（随着?因为？）越来越多的女性公开说出行业中的存在的性别歧视。

仿写：
越来越多的投资者在与企业家达成交易时迫使企业家接受某些不平等条款(Unfair term)，越来越多的企业家打破沉默，公开的说出行业中**霸王条款(“despot(专制统治者; 专制君主; 暴君)” clauses)**的问题。（句子好像有问题？）
Investors are increasingly forcing entrepreneurs to accept some unfair terms in deals with their company, as more entrepreneurs speak out about despot clauses in the industry.

[3]

(1) Lawyers and deal advisers said such clauses were being used in contracts, due diligence exercises and codes of conduct on both sides of the Atlantic, (2) following high-profile allegations of harassment and discrimination at major tech companies including Google and Uber.

(1)

  • deal advisers 投资顾问
  • due diligence(勤劳) exercises(演习，行动) 尽职调查
  • codes of conduct 行为准则
  • on both sides of the Atlantic 大西洋两岸(公司)

(2)

  • follow(ing)… 在…之后
  • high-profile(轮廓) 令人瞩目(高关注度)的
  • allegation(s) of …的指控
  • harassment and discrimination (性)骚扰和(性别)歧视
  • major tech compan(y/ies) 大型科技公司

英译中：
律师和交易顾问表示，在包括谷歌和优步在内的大型科技公司受到备受瞩目的性骚扰和歧视指控后，此类条款被纳入大西洋两岸公司的合同、尽职调查和行为守则中。

仿写：
交易顾问表示，在大型科技公司受到备受瞩目的性骚扰和歧视指控后，此类条款被纳入大西洋两岸公司的合同、尽职调查和行为守则中。

(我的) Deal adviser s said such clauses were being used in contracts, due diligence exercises and codes of conduct on the both sides of the Atlantic, following the high-profile allegation of harassment s and discrimination at major tech companies.

(正式) Deal advisers said such clauses were being used in contracts, due diligence exercises and codes of conduct on both sides of the Atlantic, (2) following high-profile allegations of harassment and discrimination at major tech companies including Google and Uber.*

[4]

(1) One investor, who declined to be named, said similar clauses had also been used by asset owners that back venture firms, such as pension funds and family offices, (2) after sexual misconduct allegations at US companies including 500 Startups, Binary Capital and Draper Fisher Jurvetson, and Israel’s Pitango Venture Capital.

(1)

  • declined to be named 拒绝(不愿意)透露姓名
  • asset owners 资产所有者
  • back… 在…之后
  • venture firms 风险投资公司
  • pension funds 养老基金会
  • family offices 家族办公室

(2)

  • sexual misconduct(行为不当) allegations(指控) 性行为不当指控

英译中：
一位不愿意透露姓名的投资者表示，在对500 Startups，Binary Capital和Draper Fisher Jurvetson等美国公司，以及以色列的Pitango Venture Capital公司提出性行为不当的指控后，同样的条款已经被风险投资公司背后的资产拥有者，如：养老基金会，家族办公室一一采用。

仿写：
一位不愿意透露姓名的记者表示，在对这家公司提起不当行为的指控后，B计划已经被这一家风险投资公司背后的资产所有者养老基金会采用。
One reporter, who declined to be named, said Plan B had also been used by asset owners that back venture firms, after misconduct allegations at this company.
